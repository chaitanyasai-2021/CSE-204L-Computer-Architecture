2. Implement lexical analyzer using C for recognizing the following tokens:

a. A minimum of 10 keywords of your choice
b. Identifiers with the regular expression : letter(letter | digit)*
c. Integers with the regular expression: digit+
d. Relational operators: &lt;, &gt;, &lt;=, &gt;=, ==, !=

f. Using files for input and output.
